Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the 'Sign Up' button on the top right corner of our website. Follow the instructions to create your account.

How do I submit a recipe?

Once you've created an account and logged in, click on the 'Submit a Recipe' button on the main navigation bar. Follow the steps to submit your recipe.
