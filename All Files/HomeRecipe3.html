Enjoy amazing Greek Orzo Salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 pack olives
  • 1 pack Feta
  • 1 pack Pine Nuts

Instructions

  1. Cut cucumber.
  2. Fry pine nuts.
  3. Put together and serve up in a bowl.
