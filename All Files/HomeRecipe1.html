Many Delicious Meals to Enjoy Made Easy

Enjoy flatbread with potatoes, onion sauce, slow-cooked pork shoulder accompanied by a yummy butternut squash and saffron orzo.

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 pork shoulder
  • 1 butternut squash
  • 1 pinch of saffron
  • 100g of orzo

Instructions

  1. Cook the potatoes.
  2. Prepare the onion sauce.
  3. Slow cook the pork shoulder.
  4. Cook the butternut squash and orzo with saffron.
