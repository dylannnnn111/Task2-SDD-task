Enjoy flatbread with potatoes, onion sauce, slow-cooked pork shoulder accompanied by a yummy butternut squash and saffron orzo.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 500g mince
  • 400g (14 oz) canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 500g Spaghetti

Instructions

  1. Cook the onion and garlic.
  2. Put the mince in.
  3. Put the tomato paste in.
  4. Cook the spaghetti and serve up
